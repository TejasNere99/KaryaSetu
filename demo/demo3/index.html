How KaryaSetu Works

For Users: Find & Hire Laborers

1. Search for Services

Enter your required skill, location, or laborer's name in the search bar.

2. Filter & Browse

Refine your search results by experience, rating, and location. Browse laborer profiles.

3. View Profiles

Check out detailed profiles, services offered, and read reviews from other users.

4. Send Job Request

Directly send a job request to your chosen laborer with your project details.

5. Communicate & Confirm

Discuss details, confirm terms, and schedule the work directly with the laborer.

6. Get Work Done & Review

Once the job is complete, leave a review to help other users.

For Laborers: Get Hired & Manage Work

1. Create Your Profile

Sign up and create a comprehensive profile showcasing your skills, experience, and services.

2. Upload Portfolio/Photos

Add images of your past work to attract more users and build trust.

3. Receive Job Requests

Get notified about new job requests from users in your service area.

4. Accept or Reject

Review job details and decide whether to accept or reject the request.

5. Manage Your Schedule

Keep track of ongoing and completed jobs through your personalized dashboard.

6. Get Reviews & Grow

Receive ratings and reviews from satisfied clients to boost your reputation.

About KaryaSetu

Welcome to KaryaSetu, your trusted platform for connecting skilled laborers with individuals and businesses in need of their services. Our mission is to bridge the gap between demand and supply in the informal labor sector, empowering both workers and users.

Our Mission

To create a transparent, efficient, and reliable ecosystem where users can easily find and hire verified laborers for various services, and laborers can find consistent work, manage their profiles, and grow their livelihoods.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital platform for blue-collar services in India, fostering economic growth, skill development, and dignified work opportunities for millions.

Our Goals

  • To provide a seamless and intuitive user experience for hiring and managing services.
  • To empower laborers with tools to showcase their skills, manage requests, and build a strong reputation.
  • To ensure trust and safety through verification processes and a robust rating system.
  • To expand our service categories and geographical reach to serve a wider community.

Our Story

KaryaSetu was born out of a simple yet profound observation: while skilled laborers are abundant, finding the right one for a specific job can often be a challenge. Similarly, many talented workers struggle to find consistent work and fair compensation. Inspired by platforms that connect professionals, we envisioned a dedicated 'setu' (bridge) for the labor force. We started with the idea of creating a digital space where transparency, reliability, and convenience are paramount. From humble beginnings, we've grown into a platform committed to transforming how labor services are accessed and provided, one successful connection at a time.
